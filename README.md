# eso

This repository contains most of the [esolang](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Esoteric_programming_language)-related projects I've worked on over the years. I've manually rewritten the git history so each directory contains what in the past might have been its own repository. Some of these are my own languages; some are implementations of others' languages; some are programs written in esolangs. I've marked the things I'm especially proud of with a star.

| Name             | About
| ---------------- | -----------------------
| ★ `faux`         | Self-hosting [FALSE](https://esolangs.org/wiki/FALSE)→x86 compiler.
| ★ `flobnar`      | [Flobnar](https://git.catseye.tc/Flobnar) interpreter in Rust.
| ★ `ruscarpone`   | [Mascarpone](https://git.catseye.tc/Mascarpone/) interpreter in Rust.
| ★ `underload`    | [Underload](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Underload) interpreter in x86.
| ★ `flurry`       | Functional version of [Brain-Flak](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Brain-Flak).
| ★ `snowmelt`     | Better Flurry interpreter in Standard ML.
| ★ `imma`         | Simpler VM with an instruction set that further encourages self-modification.
| ★ `lazyk`        | Some tools for writing [Lazy K](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Lazy_K) programs, and a solution to a halting problem challenge.
| ★ `wsbf`         | BF interpreter in [Whitespace](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Whitespace).
| `9f8`            | [9f87m4atttaaaou;](https://esolangs.org/wiki/9f87m4atttaaaou;)→JavaScript compiler in JavaScript.
| `pair`           | [Pair](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Pair)→OCaml compiler in [Tamsin](https://git.catseye.tc/Tamsin/).
| `falafel`        | Attempt at a [data-parallel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Data_parallelism) FALSE compiler in [Futhark](https://futhark-lang.org/).
| `slashes`        | Python interpreter for [///](https://esolangs.org/wiki////) written in June 2016.
| `aubers`         | [Aubergine](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Aubergine) interpreter in Rust.
| `bitq`           | [BitQueue](https://esolangs.org/wiki/BitQueue) interpreter in Rust.
| `boolet`         | Attempt at a fast [Boolet](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Boolet) interpreter in Rust.
| `digfill`        | [DigFill](https://esolangs.org/wiki/DigFill) interpreter and mediocre graphical debugger in Rust.
| `emmental`       | [Emmental](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Emmental) interpreter in Rust.
| `morbusz`        | Small self-modifying VM implemented in Zig.
| `number_factory` | [Number Factory](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Number_Factory) interpreter and compiler from BF.
| `rjam`           | Nth attempt at an efficient [CJam](https://sourceforge.net/projects/cjam/) implementation/variant.
| `rsnusp`         | Attempt at a fast [SNUSP](https://esolangs.org/wiki/SNUSP) interpreter in Rust.
| `smetana_inf`    | Fragments of a [SMETANA to Infinity!](https://esolangs.org/wiki/SMETANA_To_Infinity!) interpreter.
| `betaload`       | Underload extension providing input and some bells and whistles.
| `check`          | Language where programs switch between executing in 2d and 1d.
| `dynamite`       | Attempt at a calculus with fexprs and dynamic scoping.
| `foam`           | High-level confusing Forth.
| `full_stack`     | Reflective stack machine.
| `dreaderef`      | Simple VM with an instruction set that encourages self-modification.
| `rust_dreaderef` | Attempt at an efficient Dreaderef interpreter.
| `momema`         | Language which takes Dreaderef and makes it sane and boring.
| `metafractran`   | Filesystem-based notation for [FRACTRAN](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Fractran) programs.
| `melanjan`       | Faster [Aubergine](https://esolangs.org/wiki/Aubergine) derivative.
| `nominal-lisp`   | Attempt at a small Lisp living in a [nominal set](https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/nominal+set).
| `wdrpc`          | Programs for the [WDR paper computer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/WDR_paper_computer).
